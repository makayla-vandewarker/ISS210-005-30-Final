What is Food Insecurity all about?

By:Jack Craig on April 28, 2023

Everyone needs to eat, but not everyone has the means to.

This is the the cold hard nature of food insecurity. Not having consistent access to food and nourishment has been around since the dawn of time, and can be argued to be the driving force behind human evolution in behavior. The means of finding a consistent source of nourishment has been something societies are founded on---and die on. And this has carried forward, since the Neolithic era to the current war in Ukraine. We need food.

Me and my research team have worked to develop a summary of this issue so that everyone can get a better understanding of what specifically food insecurity is. We explore not only how this issue manifests itself, but what starts it, and what we can do. May this act as a disclaimer; we talk about something that affects millions of people around the world. We ask that you read what we have found with respect, as these are real people affected by this. We hope that you can find some of this data as enlighting as we did while searching.
