This is where you are diving into your information. Please be concise. Avoid walls of text. Only quote when you cannot possibly say it better yourself. Otherwise, you are summarizing the most relevant and key information for your audience.

Everytime you want to create a new paragraph, use these paragraph containers. For concision, you should have no more than five paragraphs and a minimum of one.

Be sure every paragraph has a main claim or takes the reader somewhere new. Then unpack that claim. What is significant? What do you want your viewer to remember? What is at stake?

If you do not want another paragraph, then simply delete ones that are unecessary.

Please be sure each post has some closure. That is, the last paragraph should be memorable. If they remember nothing else from this page, what do you hope your viewer learned?
